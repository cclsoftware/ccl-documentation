Appendix

Git Good Practice

  • Always comment your commits.

    • A single line is sufficient most of the time.

    • If you think more info on the “why” is needed, feel free do so after the first line.

    • The general rule is to either reference a ticket or another commit.

  • Commit related changes in one go.

    • Avoid unnecessary file-per-file or hourly commits.
